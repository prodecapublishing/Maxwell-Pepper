Frequently Asked Questions

Does the book cover both the Investment Advisers Act and state securities laws? +
Yes — both federal (Investment Advisers Act of 1940) and state (Uniform Securities Act) regulations are covered in depth. Understanding the distinction between federal and state rules is essential for passing the legal/ethics portion of the exam.
Is a finance degree required to understand this book? +
No. The book is written for readers with varying financial backgrounds. Core finance concepts are explained clearly. Readers with finance experience will move through some sections faster, but the book never assumes prior expertise.
Does the book include practice questions? +
Yes — review questions are included throughout, and the book recommends additional question bank resources to supplement your preparation with scenario-based practice.
