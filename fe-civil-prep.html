Frequently Asked Questions

Does this cover both the Certified Beer Server and Certified Cicerone exams? +
Yes. The book covers the material for both levels. The Certified Beer Server is the entry-level credential, while the Certified Cicerone goes deeper into brewing, styles, and service. This guide walks you through the knowledge needed for both.
Do I need to be a professional in the beer industry? +
Not at all. The guide assumes no prior beer knowledge and builds from the ground up. Whether you're a bartender, homebrewer, or simply a passionate beer drinker, you'll find the content approachable and engaging.
How long should I study before taking the exam? +
For the Certified Beer Server, most readers are ready in 1–2 weeks of casual reading. The Certified Cicerone requires more depth — plan for 4–8 weeks of dedicated study including tastings and practical experience with draught systems.
