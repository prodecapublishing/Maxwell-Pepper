Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe to use without professional experience? +
Yes. The guide is written specifically for homeowners and DIY-ers. It explains which situations you can safely handle yourself, what protective gear to use, and exactly when a problem is beyond DIY scope and requires a professional remediation company.
Does this cover black mold specifically? +
The guide addresses all common household molds including Stachybotrys chartarum (black mold). It clearly explains the difference between surface mold you can treat yourself and deeper infestations that require professional intervention.
What's in the bonus Mold-Fighting Toolkit? +
The toolkit includes a room-by-room inspection checklist, a recommended supply list with budget and premium options, a humidity monitoring guide, and a step-by-step treatment protocol you can follow immediately after reading.
