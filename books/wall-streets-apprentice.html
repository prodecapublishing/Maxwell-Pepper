Frequently Asked Questions

Is this only for the SIE, or does it help with Series 7 too? +
The book is focused on the SIE (Securities Industry Essentials) exam, which is the co-requisite for most FINRA qualification exams including the Series 7. The foundational knowledge it covers — capital markets, products, regulations — will also give you a strong base for the Series 7 and Series 63.
Do I need a finance background to read this? +
No prior finance knowledge is required. The story format introduces concepts gradually, building your understanding from scratch. The book is designed for career changers and first-timers entering the financial services industry.
How long should I plan to study for the SIE? +
Most candidates are ready in 2–4 weeks of dedicated study. The SIE is designed to test broad knowledge, not deep technical skills, so reading through this guide and supplementing with practice questions puts you in strong position.
