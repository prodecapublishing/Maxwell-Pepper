Wolric

An epic adventure of courage, sacrifice, and what it truly means to lead — with lessons that stay with you long after the last page.

"In a world where the easy path destroys what you love most, one man must choose between survival and honor — and bear the weight of both."

About This Book

Wolric is Maxwell Pepper's debut work of epic fiction — a story-driven adventure set in a world where kingdoms rise and fall on the choices of flawed but determined individuals. It follows Wolric, a soldier turned reluctant leader, through battles, betrayals, and impossible decisions that test everything he believes about loyalty, power, and what he owes the people who depend on him.

More than an adventure story, Wolric is a meditation on leadership — written in the tradition of stories that teach by immersing you in a world where the lessons have real stakes.

Themes & Lessons

⚔️ Leadership under pressure
🛡️ Sacrifice vs. self-preservation
🔥 Courage in the face of failure
🌿 Trust and betrayal
🌅 Redemption and growth
👑 The weight of responsibility

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What genre is Wolric? +
Wolric is epic fantasy/adventure fiction with strong elements of leadership and character development. It reads like a combination of historical adventure and fantasy — accessible to readers who enjoy both genres.
Is Wolric part of a series? +
Wolric stands as a complete story on its own. Future entries in the world are possible depending on reader interest. Check the Maxwell Pepper Books Amazon page for updates on any follow-up works.
Is there a Wolric poster available? +
Yes — a Wolric character poster is available in the Maxwell Pepper Shop. It's a popular item for fans of the book and makes a great piece of art that doubles as an inspiration piece.
Is this suitable for younger readers? +
Wolric is written for adult readers and mature young adults (16+). It contains themes of war, sacrifice, and moral complexity. It is not graphic or gratuitously violent, but the themes are substantive and intended for a thoughtful adult audience.
