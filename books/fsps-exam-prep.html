Frequently Asked Questions

Is this for the FS exam, PS exam, or both? +
The guide covers material relevant to both the Fundamentals of Surveying (FS) and the Principles and Practice of Surveying (PS) exams administered by NCEES. The core content applies to the FS, with deeper professional practice topics for the PS.
How are the practice exams structured? +
Both practice exams mirror the format of the actual NCEES exams — multiple choice questions covering all major topic areas. Each question includes a detailed explanation so you understand why each answer is correct.
Is this guide up to date for 2025 exams? +
Yes. This is the 2025 Edition, updated to reflect the current NCEES exam specifications for the FS and PS exams.
