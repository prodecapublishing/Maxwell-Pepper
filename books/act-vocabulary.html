Frequently Asked Questions

Is this only for the ACT, or does it help with the SAT too? +
The vocabulary is curated around ACT-specific word lists, but since the ACT and SAT draw on similar academic vocabulary pools, many of the 200+ words are equally valuable for SAT prep. If you're studying for either test, this guide will improve your verbal score.
How does learning vocabulary through a story actually work? +
Research shows that words learned in context are retained far better than words memorized from lists. When you encounter a word like "obfuscate" in a tense scene where a character deliberately misleads someone, your brain creates a rich memory around it — much stronger than a flashcard definition.
How long is the story, and can I read it casually? +
Yes — the story is designed to be genuinely readable and entertaining, not just a vocabulary delivery device. Most students finish in 3–5 hours of reading. A word glossary and review sections are included so you can check definitions without interrupting the flow of the story.
