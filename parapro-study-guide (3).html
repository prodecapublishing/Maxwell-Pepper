Who This Book Is For

This book is for working arborists, tree workers, and horticulture professionals who want to earn their ISA Certified Arborist credential — and want a study method that actually makes the content stick. If you've tried dry textbooks and found yourself zoning out, this approach is different.

What You'll Learn

  • All 9 ISA exam domains — tree biology, soil science, pruning, diagnosis, risk assessment, and more
  • Story-driven frameworks that make complex concepts memorable and easy to recall on exam day
  • Test-taking strategies specific to the ISA question format and common trick questions
  • Domain-weighted study schedules for candidates with 4, 6, 8, or 10 weeks to prepare
  • Common mistakes that cause candidates to fail — and exactly how to avoid them

Why This Book Is Different

Most arborist study guides read like reference manuals. They're comprehensive, but they're forgettable. This book uses story-driven frameworks — real-world scenarios and narrative structures — to make every domain click in a way that lasts.

It's also designed for busy adults. Short, focused chapters. Clear summaries. Structured review sections. You can make meaningful progress in 20-minute study sessions.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this book officially endorsed by the ISA? +
No — this is an independent study guide by Maxwell Pepper, not an official ISA publication. The official ISA study materials remain excellent resources. This book is designed as a complementary prep tool that makes the same content more engaging through story-driven learning.
Do I need to be an experienced arborist to use this book? +
You should have at least some professional experience in tree care — the ISA itself requires 3 years before you can sit for the exam. This book assumes basic working knowledge and builds on it rather than starting from scratch.
Is the Kindle version the same as the paperback? +
Yes — the Kindle version contains the same content as the paperback. The paperback is popular for underlining and note-taking. The Kindle version is convenient for mobile study during breaks or commutes.
What if the ISA updates their exam content? +
The ISA periodically updates its exam blueprint. Maxwell Pepper Books aims to keep study guides current with major updates. Check the Amazon product page for the latest edition details and publication date.
